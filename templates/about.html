Tarini was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every woman deserves to feel safe, confident, and empowered wherever she goes. Founded by a team of passionate technologists and safety advocates, we recognized the need for a comprehensive, user-friendly safety solution that goes beyond traditional panic buttons.

Our name "Tarini" comes from Sanskrit, meaning "The saviour" - symbolizing our mission to help women overcome safety challenges and navigate the world with confidence. We believe that technology should be beautiful, intuitive, and most importantly, reliable when it matters most.

What started as a late-night conversation between friends about personal safety has grown into a platform taking action for the safety of women. Every feature we build is designed with love,care, and delivered with the promise that we'll always have your back!

Our Mission

To create the world's most trusted and intuitive women's safety platform, combining cutting-edge technology with genuine care and community support.
